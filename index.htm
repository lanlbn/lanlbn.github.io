Libin Lan （兰利彬）


Libin Lan received his B.S. degree in computer science and technology from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, in 2008, his M.S. (Advisor: Prof. Chengliang Wang) and Ph.D. (Advisor: Prof. Chunxiao Ye) degrees in computer science from Chongqing University, in 2011 and 2021 respectively. He is currently a Lecturer with the Chongqing University of Technology, and a postdoctoral researcher with the College of Computer Science, Chongqing University, working with Prof. Zhongshi He. His research falls in the areas of (multimodal) machine learning and computer vision. His current research interests cover medical image analysis and basic visual recognition tasks such as classification, segmentation, and detection.
